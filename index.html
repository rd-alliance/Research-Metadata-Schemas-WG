About this page

This page was created in support of the outputs from the Research Data Alliance Research Metadata Schemas Working Group. Specifically, a crosswalk of schema.org properties and corresponding metadata terms. The current master list of the crosswalks collected by the RDA Metadata Schemas Working group can be found at Crosswalks from schema to schema.org. This working group and document are currently in maintenance mode as of January 2022. The tables below represent the latest update to the resource made in November 2021 and is not a live read of the original table. The page is intended as a prototype service for the research data management community in support of managers who are investigating options for including schema.org markup into existing well formed metadata.
